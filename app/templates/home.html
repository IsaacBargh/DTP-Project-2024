Welcome

Welcome to our exploration of the Southern Hemisphere's night sky! Whether you're a seasoned stargazer or just beginning your celestial journey, this website will guide you through the wonders that can be seen overhead. From brilliant stars and awe-inspiring constellations to the myths and stories that have been passed down through generations, you'll discover the secrets of the cosmos that have captivated humanity for millennia.

Here, you'll learn when to look for these fascinating objects, with viewing guides that change with the seasons. Dive into the myths and legends that have shaped human understanding of the stars for centuries, as each constellation carries a story that connects our modern gaze with the ancient past. Let us take you on a journey through space and time, revealing the beauty and mystery of the universe just above.

It is important to note that viewability of skies is based on New Zealand.
